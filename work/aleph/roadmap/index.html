Aleph Compliance · Admin / Roadmap

Four compliance modules. Six expansion verticals.

Aleph covers four regulatory modules today — FSVP, CPSIA/CPC, Prop 65, and PFAS — with six verticals identified for expansion ordered by the enforcement gradient, not by engineering ease.

Compliance modules

CPSIA / CPC

Progress: 60%

Generate Children's Product Certificates (CPC) for products intended for children 12 and under. Covers the seven required CPC fields under CPSIA Section 14(a).

Workflow
  1. Add children's product with SKU, manufacturer, country of origin, age group
  2. Upload lab test report from a CPSC-accepted lab; link to product record
  3. Complete CPC form — seven required fields (description, standards, lab info, test date/location, manufacture date/location, importer info, tracking label)
  4. Preview and generate the formatted PDF ready for filing
  • Product creation (children's product type)
  • CPC 7-field data entry form
  • CPC preview (read-only view)
  • CPC PDF generation (pdf-lib)
  • Lab test document upload + linking
  • CPC status tracking (draft → issued → superseded)
  • Product list with CPC status badges
  • Pre-populated CPSC safety standards (ASTM F963, etc.)
  • Importer info pre-fill from business profile
  • Cohort-aware product creation form
  • Server-side cohort enforcement middleware
  • CPSC e-Filing integration
  • CPC version history / supersession chain

Prop 65

Progress: 55%

California Proposition 65 toxic-substance disclosure. Assess products for listed chemicals; generate short-form and long-form warning labels at the packaging or shelf-tag size.

Workflow
  1. Add consumer product with materials, manufacturer, country of origin
  2. Answer: does this product require a Prop 65 warning? Yes / No / Not Sure
  3. Select chemicals (Lead, DEHP, Cadmium, etc.), exposure routes, warning type (cancer / reproductive / both)
  4. Generate short-form or long-form label; preview in packaging and shelf-tag sizes; download PDF
  • Yes / No / Not Sure assessment flow
  • Chemical multi-select with CAS numbers
  • Warning type selection (cancer / reproductive / both)
  • Exposure route checkboxes
  • Safe harbor compliance checkbox with explanation
  • Short-form and long-form label text generation
  • Label preview (packaging + shelf tag sizes)
  • Label PDF generation
  • Server-side chemicals endpoint (/api/prop65/chemicals)
  • Product list with disclosure status badges
  • Ownership checks on disclosure endpoints
  • OEHHA list auto-update (regulation change alerting)
  • Tailored warning label format

PFAS

Progress: 50%

Per- and polyfluoroalkyl substance disclosure for multi-state regulation (California, Maine, New York, Washington, Minnesota, Vermont, Colorado, Maryland) via a structured material disclosure questionnaire.

Workflow
  1. Add product with category, materials, manufacturer details
  2. Assessment: does this product contain PFAS? Yes / No / Unknown
  3. For each PFAS component — name, substance (PTFE, PFOS, GenX), concentration (ppm), function
  4. Select states where product is sold; auto-identify applicable regulations (CA SB 1249, ME LD 1503, NY A4978, WA HB 2658, etc.)
  5. Generate disclosure PDF with component table, applicable regulations, company certification
  • 3-step questionnaire wizard (assessment → components → regulations)
  • Yes / No / Unknown assessment with guidance
  • Dynamic component entry (add/remove rows)
  • 8-state regulation database (CA, ME, NY, WA, MN, VT, CO, MD)
  • Auto-populate regulatory basis from selected states
  • Disclosure report preview (HTML)
  • Disclosure report PDF generation
  • Product list with status badges
  • Ownership checks on disclosure endpoints
  • Expanded PFAS substance database (8,380 chemicals)
  • Manufacturer PFAS declaration request flow

Expansion verticals

Six verticals identified for next-round build, ordered by enforcement activity and overlap with the existing module stack.

Apparel & Textiles · High priority

Market: Large — #1 PFAS enforcement target.
Have: CPSIA (children's), Prop 65, PFAS.
Need: Flammability (16 CFR 1610/1615), textile labeling (fiber content, care labels).

Cosmetics & Personal Care · High priority

Market: Growing — MoCRA brand new (2023).
Have: Prop 65, PFAS.
Need: MoCRA (facility registration + product listing), FDA adverse event reporting.

Furniture & Home Goods · Medium priority

Market: Medium — formaldehyde + flammability active enforcement.
Have: CPSIA, Prop 65, PFAS.
Need: TSCA Title VI (formaldehyde), CA TB 117 (flammability).

Electronics & Electrical · Medium priority

Market: Large, technical audience.
Have: Prop 65.
Need: FCC Part 15 (EMC testing), UL/ETL listing, RoHS, conflict minerals.

Dietary Supplements · Medium priority

Market: Medium-high — FDA scrutiny increasing.
Have: FSVP, Prop 65.
Need: DSHEA (NDI notifications), cGMP (21 CFR 111).

Automotive Aftermarket · Low priority

Market: Niche.
Have: Prop 65.
Need: FMVSS, CARB, EPA emissions.
